About the Project (and us)

Telepromptu was built by a group of Rice Computer Science students. We wanted to eliminate awkward clickers and hefty screen teleprompters to make presentations more fluid for everyone, from corporate businessmen to the president. The application integrates Google Glass, Android and web apps to create one killer telepromptor for Google Glass that also enables Google Drive presentations.
